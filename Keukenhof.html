Keukenhof Tulip Gardens

Keukenhof Tulip Gardens are a very popular place to visit during about eight weeks during spring in Holland. Yearly more than 7 million flower bulbs are planted in autumn to bloom in spring. There is a variety of 800 different tulips. A unique and unforgettable experience.
